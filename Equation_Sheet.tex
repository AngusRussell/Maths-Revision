    \end{minipage}

	Jacobi: $\{\{f,g\},h\} + \{\{g,h\},f\} + \{\{h,f\},g\} = 0$\\\\
	Leibniz: $\{\{f,g\}h\} =\{\{f,h\},g\} + \{f,\{g,h\}\} $ \\ \\
    If $\{f,\mathcal{H}\}=0$ and $f$ has no explicit time dependence: $f$ is conserved
